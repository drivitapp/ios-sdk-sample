  • CompressionAlgorithm

    Please consider the libcompression documentation for further details. Short info: zlib : Aka deflate. Fast with a good compression rate. Proved itself ofer time and is supported everywhere. lzfse : Apples custom Lempel-Ziv style compression algorithm. Claims to compress as good as zlib but 2 to 3 times faster. lzma : Horribly slow. Compression as well as decompression. Compresses better than zlib though. lz4 : Fast, but compression rate is very bad. Apples lz4 implementation often to not compress at all.
